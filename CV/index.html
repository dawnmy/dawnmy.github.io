Projects

1. The function shifts driven by key microbial players in periodontitis

Using metatranscriptome, we identified several key microbial players which drive the function shifts of the community in periodontitis. These transcriptionally active microbes may give rise to the dysbiosis of the periodontal microbiome.

2. The alterations of microbiome in bacterial vaginosis (BV) during antibiotic treatment

Taking the full advantages of metatranscriptome, we investigated the temporal dynamics of vaginal microbiome from different individuals with BV during a metronidazole treatment. In this study, potential novel factors contributing to the metrinidazole resistance and BV relapse were revealed by a functional profiling.

3. The impact of stool transplantation treatment of pouchitis on human gut microbiome (on going)

By metatranscriptome, we investigated the temporal dynamics of the gut microbiome on function during stool transplantation treatment of pouchitis. This study will provide us with more knowledge about the impact of stool transplantation treatment of pouchitis on patients' gut microbiome and relapse of pouchitis.

4. Explore biologically relevant chemical space for drug discovery (2011 ~ 2013)

We proposed a novel multi-dimensional molecular descriptor termed Biological Relevance Spectrum (BRS). Several prediction models (ADME/T and biological activity) built based on BRS using SVM reach both high Precision and Recall.

5. RBRDetector: a web application for identification of RNA-binding protein residues. (2013)

6. AutoChrom3D: a webserver for modeling and visualization the 3D structure of human or mouse chromatin. (2012 ~ 2013)

7. Constructing Genome-scale metabolic network for plant pathogen provides new insights into bactericide discovery. (2013)
